Precision Valves — R. Kapoor 12 May · 11:20
We would like to clarify whether the gate valve body material specified as CF8M (ASTM A351) can be substituted with SS316 (ASTM A182 F316) for this scope. Our casting facility in Nashik is currently NABL-certified for SS316 forgings only, and procuring CF8M castings from an external foundry would add approximately 3–4 weeks to our lead time. This substitution would allow us to commit to the technical bid deadline without any schedule risk. Please advise whether this is acceptable, and if so, whether you require a third-party material certification or an in-house mill test report.
S. Mehta · Engineering Lead 12 May · 15:40
SS316 (ASTM A182 F316) is acceptable as a substitute for CF8M (ASTM A351) for this service, subject to the following conditions: (1) Mill Test Reports (MTCs) confirming compliance with ASTM A182 F316 must be submitted along with the technical bid. (2) The material traceability must be maintained from heat to finished part. (3) Any deviation from the original material specification must be clearly flagged in your Technical Bid submission document with a formal deviation note. This response applies only to gate valve bodies in this scope — check-valve body materials remain as specified.
